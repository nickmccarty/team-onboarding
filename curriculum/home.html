Overview

This series of courses is designed to take professionals, academics, and enthusiasts from the basics of time series analysis through an immersion in mean reversion- and momentum-based trading strategies before concluding with a focus on machine learning and algorithmic trading...all using Python!

Upon completion, you will walk away with a working knowledge of how to mine better insights from time series and cross-sectional datasets, which empowers you and your team to make better forecasts. Additionally, you'll learn how to leverage Python's data processing and machine learning libraries to build out your analytics pipelines, enhancing your organizational capacity to do what you do best: strategize and operationalize.
