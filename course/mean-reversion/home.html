Overview

This course will introduce you to four of the most widely-used mean reversion strategies in the past few decades. You will also learn myriad statistical techniques, such as:

  • Augmented Dickey Fuller or (ADF) Test
  • Cointegrated Augmented Dickey Fuller or (CADF) test
  • Johansen test
  • Linear Regression
  • Half-Life
  • Autoregressive models

In addition to the intuition you will develop with respect to these strategies and techniques, the provided Jupyter Notebooks will also afford you the opportunity to learn by tinkering in an interactive computational environment. By the end of this course, you will have developed a keener sense of when and how to utilize mean reversion strategies, as well as how to manage their inherent limitations. This practical approach to learning will empower you to devise and test your own mean reversion strategies, not to mention empower you to better perform time series analysis in general.
